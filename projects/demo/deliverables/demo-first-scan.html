03

Backlinks: the authority gap and how to close it

  • Links from other websites are still the strongest off-page ranking signal and a direct input to the authority that wins both search and AI citations. Acme Roastworks has 310 referring domains, 5th of 6 in this set; Globex Coffee has 4,100.
  • Volume is not the whole story. Globex Coffee's link profile carries a 28/100 spam score versus Acme Roastworks' 11/100, so a large share of that lead is low-quality directory and link-farm links, not editorial coverage. The goal is quality links, not the count.
  • We pulled the domains that link to Globex Coffee, Vandelay Roasters, Initech Brew Co. but not to Acme Roastworks. 6 are real, relevant sites worth pursuing (for example brewtrade.example, roastersguild.example, coffeereviewhub.example), and the full vetted list is handed off as a ready outreach target set. Every one it converts closes the gap above.
